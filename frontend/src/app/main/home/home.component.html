From the Presiding Elder's Desk

We give God thanks for providing us with an instrument to reach people with the message of Jesus Christ. Each church and each component ministry will actively use the Charleston District website to share the Gospel and give witness to our engaged ministries. I want to thank The Reverend Andre Thornhill, Pastor of Ebenezer, Lincolnville for spearheading the Charleston District website project. We thank him for his efforts that will only enhance the ministries of the Charleston District. We look forward to daily, weekly and monthly post related to the work of ministry and community activities. Thanks be unto God!

Blessings, Allen Parrott, PE
